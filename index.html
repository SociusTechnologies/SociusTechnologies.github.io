Socius

Socially responsible smart cities: Smarter delivery of community service through population sensing and urban technologies

Who We Are

Socius seeks to transform community service in cities. We use advanced mathematical modeling and urban science technologies to coordinate volunteer efforts and supplies in real time, and distribute them in strategic locations to help people in need.

We are currently working with the NSF Cyber-Physical Systems program, and actively looking for collaborators and partnering cities and organizations. Please contact us at contactus.socius@gmail.com if you are interested in working with us, or learning more about our project.
